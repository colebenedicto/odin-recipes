Strawberry Milk Recipe

Strawberry milk is delicious. Make this or else.

Servings: 3 500ml bottles

Preparation time: 5 mins

Cooking time: 30 mins

Ingredients

These are easy to acquire! If stawberries aren't in season yet, you may cry.

Strawberry Puree
  • Fresh Strawberries (1/2 kg)
  • Vanilla Extract (1 tbps)
  • Ground Cinnamon (1 tbps)
  • Brown Sugar (3 tbps)
Milk
  • Milk of your choice (1-2 Liters)
  • Vanilla Extract (1 tbps) - optional

Directions

dont mess this up. <3

  1. Wash Strawberries to remove dirt and commercial things that may possibly poison you (I told you to buy fresh ones).
  2. Slice Straberries into quarters and put into a bowl.
  3. Add your Brown Sugar, Vanilla Extract, and Ground Cinnamon. Mix well.
  4. Cover and leave in the fridge for 1 hour or overnight.
  5. Take off of fridge and using a fork or food smasher, smush the strawberries until pureed.
  6. Cook in meduim heat for 15 to 20 minutes. Then, take off of the heat and leave in room temperature to cool. You may also refridgerate to speed up cooling process 10 minutes after taking it off the heat.
  7. in a 500ml bottle, pour milk of your choice and add vanilla extract as desired.
  8. Pour in a quarter of your pureed strawberry.
  9. Enjoy.
